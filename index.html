ABOUT

Yash Talukdar is a theatre actor from Rewari, Haryana, who sees the stage as a life in itself—a space to explore the full spectrum of human experience without judgment. He believes acting begins with belief: live the character, let them breathe, and then—just do it.


His theatre credits include Dr. Theodor Gerard (Appointment with Death), Tyagi (Jab Sheher Hamara Sota Hai), Juror No. 3 (Jurors), and Gumashta (Muaavze). Yash is now expanding into screen work and voice projects, and is open to passionate, story-driven collaborations.
